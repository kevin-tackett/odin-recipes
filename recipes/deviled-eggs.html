Deviled Eggs

Simple and Delicious Deviled Eggs

These deviled eggs are delicious for Easter brunch and are always popular at our holiday parties. Adjust the amount of salt and sugar in this recipe to suit your taste. I like to add onion and celery for a little more texture.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Onion
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil eggs for 11 minutes. Let chill and remove shell.
  2. Cut the boiled eggs in half and remove the yolks. Reserve the whites.
  3. Mash the yolks with a fork and add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Stuff or pipe the filling into the egg white halves.
  5. Garnish with finely chopped parsley.
