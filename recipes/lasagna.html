Baked Classic Italian Lasagna

This is a beautiful layered baked lasagna. It features layers of hand made pasta, a ragu made of beef and pork, fresh bechamel, parmesan, and basil. This dish is perfect for anyone craving a warm and hearty dish. Preparation takes about 40 minutes, and baking takes another 45. This recipe will serve 4.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1/2 cup celery
  • 1/2 cup white onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 16oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 6 oz minced pork
  • 6 oz minced beef
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Finely dice and sautee the onions, carrots, celery, and garlic.
  2. Add in minced pork and beef. Sautee until browned.
  3. Add tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, add a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of the ragu sauce. Repeat this process until it reaches top of baking dish.
  5. Top with parmesan cheese and bake in a 400 degree oven for 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 20 minutes before serving.
